Queen's University Vertical Farming Team

Designing and building a functional, software-automated aeroponic vertical farm on Queen's University campus.

Aeroponic Farming

Aeroponic farming is a soilless agriculture technique that involves growing plants in a misty, nutrient-rich environment using minimal water.

Premium Produce

Vertical farming provides a controlled environment that maximizes space and resources, resulting in high-quality, premium produce that is locally grown.

Dedicated Growers

Dedicated growers are the backbone of a vertical farm, as they are responsible for ensuring optimal plant health and maximizing yield.
